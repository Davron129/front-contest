Welcome to our vocabulary test for kids! Our goal is to provide a fun and engaging way for children to learn new words and expand their vocabulary. Our team of experienced educators and designers have carefully crafted this test to ensure that it is both educational and entertaining.

We believe that learning should be a fun and enjoyable experience for kids. That's why we have designed our test to be interactive and engaging. The test features 10 randomly generated words that float on the screen with their translations. Kids can click on a word and then select its translation to score points.

We offer three levels of difficulty so that kids of all ages and abilities can participate. Our leaderboard section on the homepage is updated in real-time to show the top scorers in each level. This adds an element of competition and encourages kids to keep practicing and improving.

At the end of the test, a modal popup appears with the score, start again button, and leaderboard button. Kids can see how they did and compare their scores with others. We hope that this vocabulary test will help children to learn new words, improve their language skills, and have fun in the process.
